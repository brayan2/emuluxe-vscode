# Connect Emuluxe 

To execute simulations in your IDE, the Emuluxe engine needs securely authenticate you against your dashboard plan limits and custom environments. 

1. Click **`Login to Emuluxe`** below. A browser tab will briefly open your Emuluxe settings dashboard. 
2. Copy your unique `emx_...` Personal CLI Token.
3. Open your VS Code Settings (Command Palette -> `Preferences: Open Settings`).
4. Search for `Emuluxe` and paste your Token into the *Emuluxe: Token* configuration key.

You are now fully authenticated!
